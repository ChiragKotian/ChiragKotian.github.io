About me

IoT and Drone Specialist, Electronics and Robotics Enthusiast, Circuits and CAD Designer, Problem Solver

Experienced and passionate electronics enthusiast with a strong track record in designing and constructing intricate electronic circuits and seamlessly integrating them with microcontrollers. Proficient in developing innovative IoT projects, some of which have been selected to represent my educational institutions at the district and national levels. Skilled in working with drones and proficient in utilizing the Robot Operating System (ROS).

Extensive expertise in utilizing popular microcontrollers like Arduino, single-board computers like Raspberry Pi, and an extensive range of shields and sensors. Proficient in coding in C++ and Python to create robust and efficient solutions. Well-versed in 3D CAD modelling and 3D printing techniques. Additionally, possess solid experience with MATLAB and Simulink.

Projects I am currently working on

I like to keep working on new and novel projects as it not only keeps me engaged but also enhances my knowledge and skills.

Smart Laundry System

Make an affordable and non-invasive solution that converts a normal washing machine to an IoT one with facilty of pre-booking.

Drone-ROS Package

A comprehensive open-source ROS package that allows remote control of drones, usage of powerful ground-computer, realtime Gazeebo simulation and supports multiple drones.

Learn More

IoT Temperature and Humidity Control System for Solar Drying Chambers

Development of a simple, reliable, cost effective, IOT enabled Control System for Monitoring and Control of Temperature and Relative Humidity inside the Solar Drying Chambers. Also upload this data to cloud for remote monitoring.

Motion Controller for Games

I am working on making an IMU based gun for game which connects with the game to give users a sense of operating a real gun.

Simple Square wave inverter

Simple inverter which generates square wave AC @ 220V, 50Hz when there is power outage and charges it's 12V battery when power comes back, automatically.
